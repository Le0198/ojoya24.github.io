Get Involved with SHPE!

With more than one hundred chapter members, getting involved with a committee is easy to do. Browse through the committees below and join any upcoming meeting. If you have questions regarding any of these committees, you are welcome to email a member of the executive board.

New Member Round Table

Sundays | 5:00pm - 6:00pm

The Roundtable is the introductory committee that consists primarily of freshmen and sophomores. This group develops leadership skills within its members through community service, professional, and social events that are planned internally. All members receive a leadership position and learn more about the opportunities presented by the chapter.
